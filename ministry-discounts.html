Ministry Discounts

At Divine Printing, we believe every congregation — regardless of size or budget — deserves high-quality materials. That's why we offer special pricing for ministries.

Who Qualifies

  • Registered 501(c)(3) churches and nonprofits
  • Ministries, missions, and outreach organizations
  • Christian schools and academies
  • Youth groups and campus ministry organizations

How It Works

Mention your ministry status when requesting a quote. We'll apply appropriate discounts based on your order size and product type. Volume discounts are also available for bulk orders.

Need 500 flyers, 50 yard signs, or materials for a multi-church conference? We offer significant price breaks on large volume orders. Contact us and let's talk numbers.
