Shipping Information

Divine Printing ships orders from our Queens, NY facility to destinations across the United States.

Processing Times

  • Standard: 3–5 business days production + shipping time
  • Rush: Same-day or 1–2 business day production available on select products
  • Production times vary by product — confirm when requesting a quote

Shipping Methods

We ship via UPS, FedEx, and USPS depending on the product size and destination. Tracking information is provided for all orders.

Large Format Items

Banners, tents, backdrops, and large signage are shipped rolled or flat-packed to prevent damage in transit. Expedited shipping is available.
