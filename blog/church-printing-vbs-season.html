5 Church Printing Essentials for VBS Season

📅 March 14, 2026 ⏱ 4 min read ✝ Divine Printing
← Back to Blog

Vacation Bible School is one of the biggest community outreach opportunities of the year. A well-printed, visually exciting VBS draws kids in and tells the neighborhood something great is happening here. Here are the 5 printing essentials you need.

1. Outdoor Vinyl Banners

Nothing announces VBS to the community like a large, colorful outdoor banner. Include the VBS name, dates, age range, and your church name. Bright colors and bold fonts are key. Order at least 2-3 weeks before VBS starts.

2. Yard Signs

Place yard signs along the streets leading to your church in the week before VBS begins. Standard 18" x 24" signs on wire stakes are affordable, easy to install, and highly visible to passing traffic.

3. Flyers and Bulletins

Printed A4 or A5 flyers are still one of the most effective ways to reach families directly. Distribute them in the church bulletin, at local schools, daycares, and community centers. Include a registration QR code or website URL.

4. Stickers and Giveaways

Kids love stickers. Custom die-cut vinyl stickers with your VBS theme, bible verses, or your church logo make great daily giveaways that go home with kids — putting your church's brand in homes across the community.

5. Event Backdrop / Stage Banner

A custom backdrop banner ties the whole VBS look together and makes for great photos to share on social media — extending your reach beyond attendees.

VBS Printing Timeline

  • 8 weeks out: Finalize VBS theme and dates
  • 6 weeks out: Order outdoor banners and yard signs
  • 4 weeks out: Order flyers for distribution
  • 2 weeks out: Put up yard signs and outdoor banners
  • 1 week out: Order stickers and giveaways
