How to Choose the Right Banner Size for Your Church Event

📅 March 14, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read ✝ Divine Printing
← Back to Blog

One of the most common questions we get at Divine Printing is: "What size banner do I need?" The answer depends on where it's being displayed, how far away viewers will be, and what message you're communicating. Here's a practical guide.

Outdoor Church Banners

For outdoor use — mounted on a building facade, fence, or between poles — size matters for visibility:

  • Building facade / street-facing: 3ft x 6ft to 4ft x 8ft minimum. Viewers are typically 20-50 feet away.
  • Parking lot / fence banners: 2ft x 4ft to 3ft x 6ft work well for close-range visibility.
  • Pole banners: 2ft x 6ft is the most common standard for outdoor pole-mounted displays.

Rule of thumb: For every 10 feet of viewing distance, add 1 inch of letter height. A banner viewed from 50 feet needs letters at least 5 inches tall.

Lobby and Indoor Banners

  • Retractable roll-up banners: 33" x 79" (85 x 200cm) is the industry standard.
  • Fabric wall banners: 2ft x 5ft or 3ft x 6ft for sanctuary walls and altar areas.
  • Welcome banners: 2ft x 4ft placed at eye level near entryways.

Stage and Backdrop Banners

  • Small stage (under 12ft wide): 8ft x 4ft backdrop
  • Medium stage (12-20ft wide): 12ft x 6ft to 16ft x 8ft
  • Large stage / crusade stage: 20ft x 8ft or wider

Always measure your stage width before ordering. We print up to 10 x 20ft on a single piece.

Yard Signs

  • Standard yard sign: 18" x 24" — fits standard wire stakes, most common size.
  • Large yard sign: 24" x 36" for higher-traffic areas where more visibility is needed.
