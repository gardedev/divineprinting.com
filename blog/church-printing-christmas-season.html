Church Printing Guide for Christmas and Holiday Services

📅 March 14, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read ✝ Divine Printing
← Back to Blog

Christmas and the holiday season represent one of the highest-traffic periods for churches — many people attend services specifically at Christmas who haven't been all year. Your printed materials are often their first impression of your ministry. Make it count.

Exterior — Draw People In

Christmas Outdoor Banner

A large outdoor vinyl banner with a Christmas message — "He Has Come" or "Joy to the World — Christmas Eve Services [Time]" — is your most powerful street-level tool. Install it 3-4 weeks before Christmas for maximum exposure.

Yard Signs for Service Times

Many people drive by churches wondering "When are the Christmas services?" Answer the question with yard signs on surrounding streets. Include Christmas Eve and Christmas Day service times clearly.

Interior — Create the Atmosphere

Christmas Stage Backdrop

Transform your stage with a Christmas backdrop — a nativity scene, starry sky, "Glory to God in the Highest" scripture, or your Christmas series artwork. This single element has the biggest visual impact inside your sanctuary.

Lobby Retractable Banners

Welcome holiday visitors with themed lobby banners that set the mood from the moment they walk in. Use gold, deep blue, and white for a classic Christmas palette.

Christmas Bulletin / Order of Service

A beautifully designed Christmas bulletin is a keepsake that visitors often take home. Use premium 200-250gsm paper stock for a quality feel that reflects the significance of the service.

Outreach — Invite the Community

Christmas Invitation Flyers

Distribute door-to-door or at local businesses in the weeks before Christmas. Keep it simple: your church name, "You're invited to Christmas Eve Service," and the time and address.

Christmas Cards

Printed Christmas cards that members can personally hand out are one of the warmest, most personal forms of church outreach. Include a QR code that links to your website or event registration.

Christmas Printing Timeline

  • 8 weeks out: Finalize Christmas series theme and artwork
  • 6 weeks out: Order outdoor banner and stage backdrop
  • 4 weeks out: Order flyers, invitation cards, yard signs
  • 3 weeks out: Install outdoor banner, begin flyer distribution
  • 2 weeks out: Place yard signs
  • 1 week out: Set up lobby banners, print bulletins
