About Divine Printing

Divine Printing is a faith-inspired print shop based in Queens, New York, dedicated to serving churches, ministries, and faith communities across the country.

We were founded on a simple belief: that your message deserves to be heard — and seen. Whether you're announcing a revival, welcoming first-time visitors, or equipping your congregation with materials that reflect the glory of God, we bring your vision to life with quality, care, and purpose.

Our Location

We operate out of Queens, NY — one of the most diverse communities in the world and home to thousands of thriving churches and ministries. We know this community, and we're proud to serve it and the broader body of Christ nationwide.

What We Do

  • Church banners, flags, and fabric displays
  • Event tents, backdrops, and roll-up stands
  • Yard signs, acrylic plaques, and window decals
  • Stickers, magnets, vehicle wraps, and car signs
  • Flyers, bulletins, and event print materials

Our Promise

Every order is handled with care. We work directly with ministries of all sizes — from small congregations to large multisite churches — and we price our work fairly. Your mission matters. We take ours seriously too.
