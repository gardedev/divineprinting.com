Returns & Reprints

We stand behind our work. If your order arrives with a production defect or printing error on our part, we will reprint it at no charge.

Reprint Policy

  • Defects must be reported within 5 business days of delivery
  • Email photos of the issue to info@divineprinting.com
  • We will review and respond within 1 business day
  • Approved reprints are processed at priority speed

Customer-Supplied Artwork

We print exactly what you provide. Errors in customer-supplied files (spelling, colors, resolution) are not eligible for free reprints, though we'll do our best to catch issues before going to press.

Custom Orders

Because all products are custom-made to your specifications, we do not accept returns on correctly produced orders. Please review your proof carefully before approving.
