Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

We are based in Queens, New York. We ship orders to churches and ministries throughout the United States.

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, then click "Request a Free Quote" on any product page. Fill in your details and we'll respond within 1 business day with pricing and next steps.

Do you offer rush production?

Yes — same-day production is available on select products. Mention your deadline in the quote form and we will do everything we can to accommodate you.

Can you help with design?

Absolutely. If you don't have artwork ready, our team can help. Just let us know in the quote form and we'll discuss design options with you.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and high-resolution PNG or JPG files (300 DPI or higher). When in doubt, send us what you have and we'll advise.

Do you work with small churches?

Yes — we work with congregations of all sizes. Whether you need 25 flyers or a full event tent setup, we're here to help.

How do I check on my order?

Email us at info@divineprinting.com with your order details and we'll give you a status update promptly.
