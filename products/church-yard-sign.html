About This Product

Church yard signs are one of the most cost-effective ways to communicate with your surrounding community. Place them along the street before a special service, in members yards, or along walkways to guide visitors.

Made from 4.5mm thick corrugated plastic (Coroplast), these signs are weatherproof and lightweight. Wire H-stakes push directly into the ground — no tools required. UV-resistant printing keeps your design sharp for up to 12 months.

We offer custom cut shapes alongside standard rectangles — cross-shaped signs and directional arrows are popular choices. Full-color print on one or both sides.
