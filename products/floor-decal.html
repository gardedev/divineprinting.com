About This Product

Floor decals are an often-overlooked but highly effective form of church signage. Placed at key decision points throughout your facility, floor graphics guide visitors intuitively toward the sanctuary, restrooms, children's ministry, or welcome desk.

Beyond wayfinding, floor decals can be used creatively — a scripture verse greeting visitors at the entrance, a cross motif in the lobby, or dove-styled directional arrows leading families to the children's area.

Our floor decals include an anti-slip laminate layer for safety. They are waterproof and abrasion-resistant, suitable for entryways and interior spaces alike. Any size and shape is available.
