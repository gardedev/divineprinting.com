About This Product

Retractable roll-up banners are the workhorses of church interior signage. Set one up in your lobby to welcome first-time visitors. Place two flanking the stage to frame your sermon series. Use them at registration tables for conferences or VBS.

The beauty of roll-up banners is their speed — pull up, lock in, done in under 30 seconds. They retract just as fast and stow in a slim carry bag, ideal for multi-use spaces and traveling ministries.

We offer 80cm and 85cm widths, both at 200cm height. Same-day production is available because we know Sunday does not wait.
