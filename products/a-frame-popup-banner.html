About This Product

A-Frame pop-up banners offer maximum visual impact with minimum setup time. Their unique curved shapes stand out from rectangular banners that crowd most event spaces, drawing the eye naturally.

Ideal for placing near tables at church events, flanking a registration desk, marking a welcoming area in your lobby, or guiding visitors at conferences. Lightweight fiberglass frame makes them easy to position anywhere.

Each banner comes with a carry bag for storage and transport — great for ministries that run regular events or outreach programs and need flexible, portable signage.
