About This Product

Magnetic car signs turn your personal or church vehicle into a moving advertisement for your ministry. Every drive across town is an opportunity for your congregation to represent your church in the community — and for curious passersby to learn who you are.

Unlike vinyl decals, magnetic signs are fully removable and leave zero residue. A church member can promote the ministry during the week and remove the sign for personal use on weekends. They are also interchangeable between vehicles.

Our magnets are UV-resistant and fully waterproof, handling daily driving conditions without fading or deteriorating.
