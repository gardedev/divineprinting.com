About This Product

Magnetic car signs turn your personal or church vehicle into a moving advertisement for your ministry. Every drive across town is an opportunity for your congregation to represent your church in the community.

Unlike vinyl decals, magnetic signs are fully removable and leave zero residue. A church member can promote the ministry during the week and remove the sign for personal use on weekends.

Our magnets are UV-resistant and fully waterproof, handling daily driving conditions without fading or deteriorating.
