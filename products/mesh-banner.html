About This Product

Mesh banners are the smart choice for outdoor signage in windy conditions. Unlike solid vinyl, the open mesh structure lets wind pass through — preventing the banner from acting as a sail and reducing stress on mounting points.

Ideal for chain-link fences around your church property, scaffolding, outdoor events, or the perimeter of revival tents. Still provides bold, clear visuals from a distance while handling wind gracefully.

We offer 300gsm for lighter-duty applications and 380gsm for more permanent installations. All mesh banners include metal grommets and double-sewn edges as standard.
