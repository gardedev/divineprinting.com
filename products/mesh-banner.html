About This Product

Mesh banners are the smart choice when you need outdoor signage in windy conditions. Unlike solid vinyl, the open mesh structure lets wind pass through — preventing the banner from acting as a sail and reducing stress on mounting points.

Ideal for hanging on chain-link fences around your church property, along scaffolding, at outdoor events, or on the perimeter of revival tents. The perforated material still provides bold, clear visuals from a distance.

We offer 300gsm for lighter-duty applications and 380gsm for more permanent installations. All mesh banners include metal grommets and double-sewn edges as standard.
