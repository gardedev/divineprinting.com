About This Product

A stage backdrop banner instantly elevates the look of any event. Whether hosting a baptism, Christmas production, wedding ceremony, or multi-day conference, the backdrop appears in every photo and video frame — it should look intentional.

Our backdrop banners are printed on smooth polyester fabric and mounted on an aluminum pop-up frame that sets up in minutes without tools. The fabric pulls taut to eliminate wrinkles, giving you a clean, professional surface.

Available from 8×8ft up to 10×20ft to fill any stage. Bring your custom design — sermon series artwork, church branding, scripture passages, or seasonal imagery — and we will print it in full color.
