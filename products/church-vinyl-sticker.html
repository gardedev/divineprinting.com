About This Product

Vinyl stickers are one of the most versatile and affordable outreach tools available. A well-designed sticker with your church logo, a memorable scripture, or a bold cross graphic becomes a mini billboard wherever it is placed.

Our stickers are printed in full color on durable UV-resistant vinyl. Custom die-cutting lets us produce any shape — a traditional cross, a descending dove, a shield, a fish symbol, or your church emblem.

Stickers are extremely popular as giveaways at VBS, youth events, revivals, and community outreach programs. They are inexpensive enough to give away freely and impactful enough to leave a lasting impression.
