About This Product

Church refrigerator magnets are a classic and practical giveaway. Every time a congregation member opens their fridge, they see your church name, service times, phone number, and whatever message you choose to include.

Popular designs include weekly scripture verses, prayer request phone lines, service schedule cards, and holiday greetings. The writable surface allows members to jot notes directly on the magnet.

Available in four thickness options. Custom cut shapes — a cross, a church building silhouette, or a simple rectangle — add a distinctive touch that sets your magnets apart.
