About This Product

Acrylic and PVC signs bring a level of permanence and professionalism that fabric and paper simply cannot match. These are the signs you install once and leave — on walls, doors, welcome desks, hallways, and exterior facades.

We print directly onto the rigid material using UV printing, which bonds ink permanently to the surface. PVC is our budget-friendly option for most indoor applications. Acrylic offers premium glass-like clarity for lobby nameplates and decorative wall pieces.

Both materials can be cut to custom shapes. Cross-shaped plaques, shield crests, arched nameplates, and directional signs are all achievable. Thickness ranges from 2mm to 20mm.
