About This Product

A custom ministry flag is one of the most visible statements of identity your church can make. Whether flown from a pole outside your building, carried in a processional, or used at outdoor crusades, a flag bearing your church emblem broadcasts your mission with dignity.

We print on 75D or 100D polyester — a durable, weather-resistant fabric that holds color well and flies cleanly in the wind. Standard size is 3x5ft, but we produce any size you need.

For ministries wanting visibility from both sides — processional or event flags — we offer double-sided printing with a center liner to prevent show-through.
