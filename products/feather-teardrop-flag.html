About This Product

Feather and teardrop flags are designed to be noticed. Their distinctive curved shapes create movement in even the lightest breeze, drawing the eye from a distance and signaling that something is happening at your location.

Perfect for outdoor events — place them along the sidewalk before a revival, flank your ministry tent at a festival, or line the entrance to your church for special services. Visible up to 5.5 meters tall.

Each flag comes with a fiberglass pole and your choice of base: a ground spike for grass, or a cross base for indoor and hard surface use. Assembly takes under a minute with no tools.
