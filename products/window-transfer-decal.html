About This Product

Transfer decals give your church windows and glass doors a clean, permanent-looking display without the boxed-in appearance of a standard sticker. Because there is no background film — just the lettering and graphics — the result looks like text is printed directly on the glass.

Ideal for displaying your church name on the front door, listing service times on a window, applying scripture to a glass partition, or labeling ministry rooms with elegant vinyl lettering.

At 100 microns thick, our transfer decals are durable enough for outdoor glass while remaining cleanly removable if your messaging ever needs to change.
