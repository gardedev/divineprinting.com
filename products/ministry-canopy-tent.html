About This Product

A branded canopy tent is your ministry mobile billboard. Whether at a community fair, block party, farmers market, or public outreach event, your tent is the first thing people see — a fully branded tent tells them exactly who you are.

Our aluminum-framed canopy tents are built for repeated outdoor use. The pop-up frame sets up quickly and holds firm in typical outdoor conditions. The polyester canopy and optional sidewalls can be printed in full color.

Three sizes available — 10x10ft for small presences, up to 10x20ft giving you a full command post for larger events with room for tables, chairs, and ministry materials.
